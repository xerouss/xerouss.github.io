Space Puck

C#, Unity

Originally created in a University game jam and polished afterwards in a team of 7 people (3 artists, 3 programmers and 1 sound engineer). Space Puck is a local multiplayer arcade game for two people. Fight for victory and your life in 5 game modes. Score points by hitting the puck in the other player's goal and the player with the highest points when the time runs out wins.
