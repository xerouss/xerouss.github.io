Planet 9

C#, Unity

Created during my placement year in a self-employed games company called Delta Dawn Games. Created with 2 other programmers. Planet 9 is a rogue-like game where you have one life and you must go through portals to get to the next levels to reach the final boss. Planet 9 includes features such as:

  • Procedural generation
  • AI pathfinding
  • Power ups
  • And More!
